---

This is about as good as the original Half Life, with all of the great story, environment and game play including the weapons — especially your trusty crow bar. It's all in this game, and is a blast.

Bonus for allowing me to enable the joystick for main game play, but for shooting, there really is no equal to the precision of a mouse and keyboard. It is more fun to use the joystick, but you suffer on accuracy of placement of the cross hairs for longer range shots.

**Highly recommend this game.**
