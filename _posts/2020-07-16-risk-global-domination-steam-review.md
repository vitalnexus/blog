I've only played this game a short time, and only in Single Player, so I'm going to focus on that section only.

First, I tried the maps that are "Free this week." They were prison layout maps, and were a nice change from the commonly known world map of the board game origin. The cost to play each game is 5 tokens, and you get around 200 to start. By the time the game is over, you've already gained back 2 or 3 tokens, since they give you 1 every 20–30 minutes of being in game.

The AI opponents don't seem to have too much of a change in strategy from Easy to Expert, and I've already defeated 5 AI on Expert mode. That said, I still love this game — the only downside to playing it on a physical game board was moving the pieces, and the dice knocking pieces off countries, not having enough room on each country for your troops.

Now that it is online, I really enjoy the challenge and strategy this game provides. The interface and speed of the controls and game is awesome!
