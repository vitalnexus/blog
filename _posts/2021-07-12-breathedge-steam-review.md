This game kept my interest and I really enjoyed the sense of humor throughout. Not giving out spoilers, but there is a reference to a great game trilogy which I have also played through to completion more than once.

Anyway, so far I've completed this game twice. I also made it about 32 hours on Impossible, and I'm planning to do another attempt at it soon. Thinking of creating a guide for the game on Steam as my first ever created guide — comment if interested.

I highly recommend this game if you don't mind collecting resources as you uncover a very unusual story.
